Lycoperdon perlatum , popularly known as the common puffball, warted puffball, gem-studded puffball, or the devil's snuff-box, is a species of puffball

Taxonomy

The species was first described in the scientific literature in 1796 by mycologist Christiaan Hendrik Persoon.[3] Synonyms include Lycoperdon gemmatum (as described by August Batsch in 1783[4]); the variety Lycoperdon gemmatum var. perlatum (published by Elias Magnus Fries in 1829[5]); Lycoperdon bonordenii (George Edward Massee, 1887 [6][2]); and Lycoperdon perlatum var. bonordenii (A.C. Perdeck, 1950[7]).[1][2]
